About Arman Nadjarian's Text Analyzer website:

Hello, welcome to my About Page:

This website began as an exercise in webpage construction and the introduction of basic algorithms via Django. Upon completion of this project, I decided to further improve upon it through various stylistic improvements (via implementation of CSS) and the addition of vowel/consonant counting.
